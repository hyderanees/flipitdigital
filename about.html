Our story

Built by people who got scammed once too many times.

We started FlipIt because the digital economy was overflowing with valuable assets — and overrun with scams. We built the safety net it always needed.

Our mission

Make digital trade as safe and trustworthy as buying from Apple or Amazon — but for the goods real people actually trade in 2026: aged accounts, AI subscriptions, in-game inventories, gift cards, and the long tail of digital products invented every week.

We do this with three pillars:

Escrow first

Money never moves until trust is proven.

Verified humans

Every seller is KYC'd. No fakes.

Real moderators

24h human dispute resolution.

Our journey

From 2024 frustration to 2026 marketplace.

2024

The painful start

Founder Haider lost $2,400 in three back-to-back digital scams (an Instagram page, a "Discord trader", a sketchy Telegram seller). Sketched the FlipIt concept on a napkin.

2025

MVP & Phase 1

Launched a closed beta with 50 sellers. Built core escrow, KYC, and dispute system. First $100K of GMV traded with zero fraud loss.

Q1 2026

Wallet & messaging

Multi-currency wallet, in-app messaging with file sharing, and mobile apps for iOS and Android.

Q2 2026

AI fraud engine + scaling

Released our behavioral fraud engine. Fraud loss rate dropped to 0.6%. Crossed 120K verified members and $12M cumulative GMV.

Now

Going global

Expanding seller verification to 28 countries, adding 12 more local payment methods, and launching the FlipIt Pro API for high-volume merchants.
