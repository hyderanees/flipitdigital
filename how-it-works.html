Escrow that protects every cent.

Your money never touches the seller until you confirm delivery. If anything goes wrong, our team intervenes within 24 hours.

1

Seller verification & listing

Every seller passes ID + liveness checks before listing. High-value categories require additional review and source-of-asset proof.

  • Government-issued ID
  • Selfie liveness check
  • Listing source proof (account history, license proof)

If something goes wrong

Disputes resolved within 24 hours.

Real humans investigate every dispute. We side with whoever has proof — not whoever shouts loudest.

1. Open dispute

One click on the order page opens a dispute. The escrow remains frozen.

2. Both parties submit proof

Screenshots, recordings, chat history, and the FlipIt audit log are auto-attached.

3. Human moderator reviews

A trained dispute agent reviews within 24h. They reach out for missing info if needed.

4. Resolution & refund

If you're owed a refund, it's issued within 30 minutes — straight back to the original payment method.
