Built for trust

Five layers of protection on every order.

From identity verification to AI-powered fraud detection, we engineered FlipIt to be the safest place in the world to trade digital assets.

Layer 1 · Escrow Vault

Every payment enters our PCI-compliant escrow vault and stays frozen until you confirm the asset works. Funds are segregated from operating capital and never co-mingled with company funds.

Layer 2 · KYC verification

Every active seller passes ID + selfie + liveness checks via Persona / Onfido. High-value sellers face source-of-asset reviews.

Layer 3 · Fraud detection

Behavioral analytics, device fingerprinting, and IP intelligence flag bots, multi-accounting, and laundering attempts in real time.

Layer 4 · Human moderation

Trained dispute agents review every disputed order within 24 hours. Decisions follow a published evidence policy.

Layer 5 · Continuous monitoring

Suspicious-activity rules monitor every transaction. Withdrawal approvals add a final human gate above $1,000.

Buyer protection

If it's not as described, you don't pay.

  • Refund-on-revoke for accounts & subscriptions
  • Partial refund if inventory snapshot doesn't match
  • 100% refund for invalid codes (within 24h)
  • 14-day window for high-value gaming accounts
  • Funds returned to original payment method in 30 min
Seller protection

Get paid. No chargebacks. No drama.

  • FlipIt absorbs payment-network chargebacks
  • Auto-confirm if buyer doesn't act in window
  • Buyer-side fraud detection blocks bad actors
  • Tamper-proof chat & delivery audit trail
  • Instant payout to wallet on order release
Category-specific safeguards

Specialized protection for every kind of asset.

Social media accounts

Engagement audit, age verification, and a 14-day recovery window. If the platform recovers the account, you get refunded.

Gaming accounts

Inventory snapshots via Riot, Valve, and Mojang APIs. Email-change handoff via FlipIt secure tunnel.

AI subscriptions

Auto-renewal alerts, expiry tracking, and refunds when invites are revoked or sessions removed.

Gift cards & codes

Real-time validation against issuer APIs. Auto-refund if a code is reported invalid within 24h.

Growth services

30-day drop guarantee. Followers below threshold get topped up at no extra cost.

Creative software

License-pool monitoring detects revoked seats and auto-issues replacements from inventory.

Zero tolerance policy

What we never allow.

FlipIt exists to make digital trade safe and legal. The following are blocked at signup and result in permanent bans + asset forfeiture.

Stolen accounts or hacked credentials
Carded or fraudulent payment items
Bot followers or fake engagement
Money laundering / structuring
Cracked / pirated software keys
CSAM, illegal goods, weapons
