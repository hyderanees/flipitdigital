Terms of Service

Last updated: May 2026

These Terms of Service ("Terms") govern your access to and use of FlipIt Digital ("FlipIt", "we", "us"), the digital asset marketplace operated at flipitdigital.com. By creating an account or using FlipIt, you agree to these Terms.

1. The FlipIt Service

FlipIt is an escrow-protected marketplace where verified users buy and sell digital assets including (but not limited to) social media accounts, growth services, gaming accounts and inventories, gift cards, software subscriptions, and AI tools. FlipIt holds buyer payments in escrow until delivery is confirmed.

2. Eligibility & Accounts

You must be at least 18 years old (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction) to use FlipIt. You are responsible for keeping your credentials secure. We may suspend or terminate any account that violates these Terms or our Acceptable Use policy.

3. Verification (KYC)

Sellers and high-value buyers must complete KYC, which includes government-issued ID and a selfie liveness check. We use third-party processors (Persona, Onfido) and may request additional documentation for source-of-asset reviews.

4. Listings & Prohibited Items

You may only list assets you legally own and are entitled to resell. Prohibited items include — without limitation — stolen accounts, hacked credentials, fraudulently obtained codes, cracked software, illegal goods, items that violate platform terms of service in a manner that creates buyer risk, and anything restricted under applicable law.

5. Escrow & Payments

When you make a purchase, your payment enters our segregated escrow vault. Funds are released to the seller after you confirm delivery, the protection window expires, or a dispute is resolved in the seller's favor. We may charge transaction fees as described in Pricing.

6. Disputes

If a transaction goes wrong, you must open a dispute within the protection window. Disputes are reviewed by a human moderator within 24 hours. The decision is binding and based on our published evidence policy.

7. Refunds

Refunds are issued to the original payment method when (a) a code is invalid, (b) an account is recovered by its original owner within the protection window, (c) a subscription invitation is revoked, or (d) a moderator rules in favor of the buyer in a dispute.

8. Fees

We charge a commission of 5–8% on successful sales depending on the seller tier. Optional add-ons such as featured listings and newsletter spotlights have separate fees. All current fees are listed at flipitdigital.com/pricing.

9. Withdrawals

Sellers can withdraw to bank accounts, supported wallets, or crypto. Large withdrawals may require additional verification. We comply with applicable AML / KYC regulations and may report suspicious transactions to authorities.

13. Termination
