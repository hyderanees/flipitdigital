How escrow works

Safe by design. Simple by default.

Every order is held in escrow. The seller delivers, you confirm — only then are the funds released. No middlemen drama, no chargebacks, no scams.

1

Seller lists

Verified seller posts a digital asset with proof.

2

Payment is locked into FlipIt escrow vault.

3

Delivery

Codes, accounts or files are transferred.

4

Confirmation

Buyer confirms within the protection window.

5

Release

Funds are paid out — minus a small fee.
